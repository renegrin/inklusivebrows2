Care during your session

Consultation (15 minutes): When you arrive, we will review your consent forms, your daily routine and lifestyle. We will go over what your brow goals are and feel free to bring a inspirational brow picture as a reference. I will be able to walk you through the whole process and answer any questions that you may have before the next step.

Brow Mapping (35 minutes): We then create the shape of your brows based on your bone structure and your facial features. We use measuring tools, and an app to ensure that we create as much symmetry to your face as possible. Once you approve the shape, it's time to numb your brows!

Microblading (1.5 / 2 hours): Now you’re ready for the actual treatment! We start creating each hairstroke and implant pigment on the uppermost layer of your skin. We use a sterile disposable handheld tool made with microneedles . This is done by following the natural growth of your brow hairs. To achieve the most natural results, the hairstrokes may differ based on that particular brow.

Aftercare: You may have fallen asleep but you finally made it!!! Your brows are ready for the final reveal. We will review your aftercare instructions and provide a kit that has everything you need to ensure your brows heal beautifully. You will also receive a card that walks you through each stage of the healing process starting on day 1 through day 28! It is very important to follow the instructions given to you. We would hate to waste your investment and all this time that it took to create them.
