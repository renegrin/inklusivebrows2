YOUR LOOK
Starts at your eyes

INKLUSIVE BROWS enhances the beauty of your brows by improving their shape, color, and density using microblading and micro-shading. Eyebrows are the most distinguishing feature of the face. Even minor changes have a significant impact on your appearance.  Get started with an appointment with INKLUSIVE BROWS to get the look you've always desired.

OUR SERVICES

The following are the services we offer. Prices are subject to change.

MICROBLADING

Microblading is realistic hair-like strokes which is created by a handheld disposable tool, making your eyebrows look naturally full with shape. This procedure is compatible for normal to dry skin. Oily skin can receive this treatment, but the ink retention may result in multiple touchups.

410$

LASH LIFT & TINT

Lifts and curls your lash from base to tip using a perm solution, and tinting the lashes with a special dye that gives you weeks of dark full lashes without using mascara. Lash lifts last for 4-6 weeks, tint lasts 3-4 weeks. 

110$

MICROBLADING & MICROSHADING

This combination of microblading and shading creates more depth and fullness using a handheld tool for the hair strokes, and a shading machine for the fullness. This procedure is compatible for all skin types and is perfect for people with less hair. 

460$

TOUCH-UP

This perfection session is to touch up areas where the skin did not take the pigment. If you are past the 6 month mark, please book 7-12 month touchup. If you booked Microblading only and would like to add shading, it will be an additional $50 at the time of appointment.. 

110$
