What is Microblading?

Microblading is the most innovated beauty treatment in the Permanent Makeup industry today. It is a semi-permanent technique, using the highest quality Phi pigments applied into the surface layer of the dermis. Color pigment is chosen to match your natural hair color, and the hair strokes are drawn in the direction of your natural hair growth, creating the most natural results possible. Once you complete your initial session and your 6-8 week touchup, your new brows will last up to 3 years, with minor touch-up sessions to refresh pigment color over time.

Why a Phibrow Artist?

A Phibrow artists produce the highest quality of work due to their rigorous 6 month training course, versus the standard 2-3 day live training. The Phi-Academy has a total of 11 levels that are required to pass in order to receive their certification, which ensures their work is held at a very high standard.

Phibrow artists are trained to produce very precise hair strokes, symmetry, and a perfect shape using the Golden ratio method. All pigments, tools, and blades are Phibrow products which have been tested time and time again to ensure the highest quality, and to avoid any dangerous heavy metals found in pigment.

If you are wanting great results with your microblading treatment, it is highly recommended to seek out a Phibrow artist.
