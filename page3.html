Care after your session

Day 1: Wipe brows gently every hour with soaked cotton round and pat dry with a clean tissue, then apply a rice grain size layer of Skincandy.

Day 2: Follow the same instructions as day 1 but only 2-3 times.

Day 3: No more washing!!! Keep brows as dry as you can. Apply Skincandy 2-3 times a day if you have dry skin. Apply Skincandy 1-2 times if you have oily skin.

IMPORTANT REMINDERS
During the first 7 days:
Avoid all creams (except for protective cream). Avoid applying makeup on the eyebrow region. Avoid workouts that involve excessive perspiration, saunas, facial massages and steaming.

In the next 30 days:
• Avoid sunbathing
• Avoid tanning beds
• Avoid light therapy
• Avoid chemical peels,
• Avoid fruit acids
• Avoid microdermabrasion
• Avoid creams that contain regeneration factors.

Always avoid laser treatment over the treated area (Fraxel laser, IPL). Use of antibiotics and hormonal therapy can lead to faster pigment fading.

Always protect the eyebrows from the sun using an SPF (do not apply an SPF in the first 7 days). Avoid sleeping on your side to prevent fading. Sleep with a clean pillow case.
